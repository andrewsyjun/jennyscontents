Jenny's Contents

Privacy Policy

Jenny's Contents is an internal content strategy tool for organizing owned social media account data and planning real estate content.

If you authorize a social platform integration, the app uses the granted access only to read the account profile, owned media metadata, and performance metrics needed for content planning. Access tokens are stored locally by the operator and are not sold or shared.

To revoke access, remove the app from the connected platform account settings and delete the local environment credentials.
