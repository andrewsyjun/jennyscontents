Jenny's Contents

Data Deletion Instructions

Jenny's Contents is an internal planning tool for Jun Residential Group. The public review build stores Facebook Login access only in the current browser session. Closing the browser tab or clearing browser site data removes that review session token.

To remove connected platform access, open the relevant platform account settings and remove Jenny's Contents from connected apps and websites. For Facebook or Instagram, remove the app from Meta account app settings, then delete any local `.env` credentials used by the private operator build.

Generated content strategy files can be deleted directly from the local project folder or the connected Google Drive Content folder.
